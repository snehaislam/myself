About Myself

I was born in Dhaka in 2005. I come from a small and supportive family consisting of my father, mother, and younger sister. My father is a businessman, and my mother is both a businesswoman and a working professional. My younger sister is currently a student.

From my childhood, I have been actively involved in various co-curricular activities alongside my studies. I have experience in singing, dancing, scouting, recitation, drama, advertising, and art. Alhamdulillah, through dedication and hard work, I have achieved many things in different fields. I have always tried to balance my academic life with my passions and interests.

I believe all the credit for who I am today goes to my mother, whose support, guidance, and encouragement have shaped my journey. I am ambitious and determined to go far in life. My biggest dream is to make my parents proud and bring honor to my family. Let’s hope for the best.
